Echoes of OreBit 

A procedurally generated space mining game where you play as a deserted survivor and must collect debris and resources to upgrade and refuel your ship to continue your journey through the galaxy.

-Seeded procedurally generated environment leaving unlimited room to explore
-mining system to collect resources on your way around
-upgrade systems to speed up your ship and improve attributes 
-High efficiency save and load system

How to play
-Clone the repository
-Build and Open the game
-Navigate the menu for desired options
-visit the star map to find a system to play in 
-WASD to move around, space to shoot,  ‘I’ for inventory, and Esc for the menu
-upgrade and refuel your ship in the menu

Limitations
-Single player only
-PC only
-limited gameplay
